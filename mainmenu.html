KEYWORDS

A list words from the game you may not have known:

Catcalling- a form of harassment. It is when a person makes a whistle, shout, or comment of a sexual nature to a woman passing by.

Objecification - treating a person as a thing. Sexual objectification is the act of treating a person as an instrument of sexual pleasure.

'slut' shaming - form of social stigma applied to people who are believed to violate traditional expectations for sexual behavior, mainly for women and girls.

wage gap - a statistical indicator often used as an index of the status of women's earning relative to men's.

sexualization- to make something sexual in character or quality, or to become aware of sexuality, especially in relation to men and women. Often linked to sexual objectification. It occurs when "individuals are regarded as sex objects and evaluated in terms of their physical characteristics and sexiness."

microaggressions - a term which some use to refer to unintended discrimination.

Body shaming - inappropriate negative statments and attitutes toward another person's weight or size.

About Us

The Squad for Social Change are Girls Who Code students. I Woke Up Like This is our final project. We made it to bring awareness to issues women face on a daily basis. We want as many people to understand the point-of-view of women around the world.
